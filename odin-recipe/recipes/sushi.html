Sushi

Yummy for the tummy!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Crab Meat
  • Seaweed

Steps:

  1. Spread seasoned sushi rice over a sheet of nori, leaving 1 inch at the top.
  2. Add fillings (fish, avocado, cucumber) in a line across the center.
  3. Roll tightly using a bamboo mat, seal with water, then slice into pieces.
